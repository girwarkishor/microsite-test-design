The eligible Referrer shall be eligible to get Referral Points (whose validity is 365 days from the date of its first generation, after which it expires on first generate first expire basis), which will be 15% of the cumulative brokerage generated by his Referee.


Consolidated Referral Points of current month will credit to Referrer on every 25th of next month.

  • 1 Referral Point = 1 Indian Rupees, if Referrer opts to transfer Referral Points to Equity / MF ledger.
